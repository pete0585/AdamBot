## Executive Summary

Adam Peters has built a significant and growing presence in the veteran transition space — 158 published podcast episodes, multiple magazine features, conference speaking engagements, three active ventures (The Strategic Veteran, Alset Technologies, Amakaya Retreat), and a strong LinkedIn following. However, thestrategicveteran.com dramatically under-represents this reality. The website is essentially a static brochure from mid-2024 that has not kept pace with Adam's rapid evolution as a thought leader, tech founder, and public speaker. This audit identifies every gap between what the world knows about Adam Peters and what his website communicates.
